Chessboard Overview

  • The Grid and Notation: The chessboard consists of 64 squares arranged in an 8x8 grid from a1 to h8. The squares alternate between light and dark colors. A key rule is that the bottom-right square must always be a light square (h1).

  • Files, Ranks, and Diagonals:
    • Files: The vertical columns of squares, labeled 'a' through 'h'.
    • Ranks: The horizontal rows of squares, labeled '1' through '8'.
    • Diagonals: Lines formed by squares of the same color corner-to-corner.
